About Me

Greetings, All! My name is Steven Shirley and I am a freelance percussionist, as well as a full time member of the Dallas based band Michael and The Influence. I am also an illustrator and have created many observations; primarily with acrylic on canvas. I graduated from Tarleton State University in December 2017 with a major in Psychology and a minor in General Business; however, I am currently in SMU's post-graduate coding program with focus to become a full time web developer. Hobbies of mine include: Drumming, Illustrating, Studying European Culture's History and Archetecture, Computer Language (primarily HTML and CSS so far; it's so fun though, it's becoming a hobby!), Biking, Fishing, Hunting, Hanging With My Dog, and hopefully coming soon- Working Out At The Gym.
